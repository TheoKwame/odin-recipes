Lasagne


Description

Lasagne is a traditional Italian dish made of layers of flat pasta sheets with meat or vegetables and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, and sugar.
  6. Season with basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and egg.
  9. Spread a portion of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Layer half of the noodles over the sauce.
  11. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  12. Top with half of the mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  13. Repeat the layers once more.
  14. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
