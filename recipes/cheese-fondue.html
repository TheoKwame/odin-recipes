Cheese Fondue


Description

Cheese fondue is a traditional Swiss dish made of melted cheese served in a communal pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound grated Gruyère cheese
  • 1 pound grated Emmental cheese
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the wine and garlic over low heat until warm.
  2. Add the grated cheeses and stir until melted and smooth.
  3. Stir in the lemon juice and nutmeg.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately in a fondue pot with bread cubes or vegetables for dipping.
